Pour over Coffee

Ingredients

  • 150g Freshly Ground Coffee Powder
  • 1 litre Hot Water
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Get an Pour Over Flask and Lay the Coffee Filter and wet it with boiling water
  2. Step 2: Add the Coffee Powder and let it rest for 30 secs
  3. Step 3: Add 250ml water in 30 second intervals over the coffee for 90 seconds
  4. Step 4: Wait for the coffee to drip to the bottom and remove the filter and enjoy with or without sugar per your preference

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Waiting Time: 3-5 minutes

Total Time: 10 minutes
